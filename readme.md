# Buas intake project

## How does this game work?
The customer will request an item. To get this item you will need to combine several items inside
the cauldron, up its temperature by moving the blower up and down and stirring the pot. The item
will automatically move to your inventory and you can give it to the customer by interacting with them.
During the process you can also buy animals and put them in their respective pens for more item ingredients.

## How do i put items in the cauldron?
Stand near the cauldron, press E to open the inventory and drag the item you want in the cauldron
out of the inventory.

## How do i know the recipe of the item?
The CraftingRecipes image in this folder provides you with all the recipes in the game

## Keybinds
Interact - Enter
Close menu/Open settings - Escape
Open animal shop - T
Open inventory - E
Move character - W/A/S/D
Reset cauldron - R
